## üé≤ Risk Assessment

### Marketing Risks:
- **Developer skepticism** - Need proof, not promises
- **"Just another tool" fatigue** - Must emphasize unique speed advantage
- **Free alternative preference** - Position as premium but cost-effective
- **Feature-first mindset** - Focus on business value over technical specs

### Mitigation Strategies:
- **Open source the benchmarking** tools
- **Provide free tier** for individual developers
- **Share real performance data** transparently
- **Build in community** before monetizing
